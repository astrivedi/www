Trustworthy Reasoning and Intelligence

Our research group explores the foundations and applications of trustworthy AI, formal methods, and learning-enabled systems...

Research Themes

  • Formal Methods for Reinforcement Learning (RL) – Bridging logic, automata, and RL to synthesize policies with guarantees
  • Trustworthy Reasoning with LLMs – Integrating symbolic reasoning and LLMs for verifiable explanation and decision-making
  • AI, Software, and Accountability – Studying fairness, compliance, and legal aspects of data-driven software
  • Secure and Safe CPS – Designing verifiably correct controllers for safety-critical cyber-physical systems
