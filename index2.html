About My Research

My research focuses on enabling rigorous system engineering of data-driven systems to improve safety, privacy, fairness, and accountability. These systems—often found in critical settings like self-driving cars, medical devices, and criminal justice—learn from data and evolve, making traditional software guarantees insufficient. I aim to redefine the role of formal methods in this changing landscape by bridging them with inductive and adaptive techniques from AI and machine learning.

Areas of recent interest include:

  • Reinforcement learning under logical constraints
  • Fairness-aware configuration of ML libraries
  • Legal compliance and correctness in socio-technical systems like tax software
