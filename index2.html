About

I am an Associate Professor at the University of Colorado Boulder. My research focuses on formal methods, reinforcement learning, AI safety, and explainable systems. I aim to bridge the gap between theory and the evolving demands of data-driven AI systems.

Selected Projects

Explainable Formal Methods with LLMs and SMT Solvers

Developing proof-decomposing and explanation-generating tools that combine large language models and SMT solvers to ensure trustworthiness in AI decisions.

Reinforcement Learning for Mean-Payoff and Omega-Regular Objectives

Novel algorithms that reduce complex objectives in stochastic control to average-reward RL, improving learnability and interpretability.

Fairness Verification and Debugging in ML

Design patterns and automated tools for identifying and correcting fairness violations in ML pipelines.
