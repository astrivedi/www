Project Overview

We are seeking two motivated students to contribute to a research project at the intersection of computational cardiovascular modeling and reinforcement learning. The project focuses on developing learning-enabled control strategies within cardiac digital twin models for adaptive decision-making in complex cardiovascular physiology.

  • Work with cardiac digital twin simulation environments
  • Design and run computational experiments
  • Analyze results and assist with technical documentation

  • Background in machine learning, reinforcement learning, control, or numerical methods

Preferred Qualifications

  • Familiarity with scientific computing tools (NumPy, PyTorch, etc.)
  • Interest in biomedical systems or cyber-physical systems
